# Code of Conduct

This project adopts the **Contributor Covenant 2.1** as its code of
conduct.

The canonical text is published at
<https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/>.

In short: be respectful, assume good faith, and keep discussion
focused on the project. Maintainers may remove comments, commits,
code, edits, or PRs that don't follow this standard, and may ban
contributors whose behavior is repeatedly out of line.

## Reporting concerns

Concerns about contributor behavior can be raised by opening a
private message to the repository maintainer via GitHub. Reports
will be reviewed and handled confidentially.
